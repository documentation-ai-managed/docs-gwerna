## Quick Start

1. Log in to your Gwerna account.
2. Click **New Project** on your dashboard.
3. Enter a project name and optional description.
4. Click **Create**. Your project will appear on the projects list.

## Next Steps

- Add team members from the project page.
- Start adding documentation sections for your project.

<Callout kind="tip">You can edit or archive a project anytime from its settings.</Callout>
