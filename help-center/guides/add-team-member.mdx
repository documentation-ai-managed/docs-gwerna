---
title: Adding a Team Member
description: Invite team members to your Gwerna project.
---

## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Go to your project dashboard in Gwerna.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Team tab">
    Click the **Team** tab on the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite member">
    Enter the email address and click **Invite**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage roles">
    Assign appropriate roles to newly added members from the team list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
